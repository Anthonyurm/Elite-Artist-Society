Questions Answered

What exactly happens after I join?
The moment we say yes, you get instant access to the full platform, the Elite Artist Society, the Elite Playbook, weekly coaching calls, every framework, and all our EAS templates. Within a few days, you have your personal 1:1 kickoff with Anthony to map your plan.
How much time do I need each week?
Most artists spend 5 to 10 hours per week between learning, creating, posting, reviewing feedback, and testing new content. Minimum is 4 to 6 focused hours. But it is kinda like joining a gym and asking how long until you get a six pack. It depends on how much consistent effort you put into it.
Do you guarantee results?
We don't guarantee a specific follower count, revenue number, or brand deal. What we do guarantee is a structured process, expert feedback, and the tools to help you improve faster than figuring it out alone. In the first 30 to 60 days, we typically look for clearer positioning, stronger hooks, better retention, more consistent output, and improved audience quality. Compounding growth usually becomes visible in months 2 to 3, depending on your starting point and consistency.
What if I am starting from a small audience?
Starting smaller can actually be cleaner, less to undo. As long as your music is genuinely good and you are willing to post consistently, the system works. We have helped artists go from a few thousand real followers to engaged, paying communities. The size of the room matters less than what you do with the people in it.
Is this a course or a mentorship?
A mentorship. You get a structured learning platform as the foundation, then weekly live calls, direct feedback in the community, and a clear path the whole way through. You are never handed videos and told good luck. You always have a next step and a person to ask.
